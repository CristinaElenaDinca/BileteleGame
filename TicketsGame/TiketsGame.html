The Tickets Game

When you have a day out with friends, take a run with this Tickets Game. It's an alternative to the classic board game.

Let's get familiar with the rules!

  1. This is a game ment to be played with your friends. It's a team game so you would need at least 4 friends to play one game (there is no maximum limit. The more the merrier).
  2. Every player from each team will take turns (1 from team A, 1 from team B, 2nd from team A, 2nd from team B a.s.o.).
  3. You would need a laptop/tablet and each player will take turn as the game shows on screen.
  4. You will have 20 seconds. Get your team to guess as many words as possible in 20 seconds. The team with as many words guessed will be the winner.
  5. REMEMBER: when you are up, you are the only one allowed to see the screen and only your team can guess!
